Executive Summary

Several instances of missing title and/or role attributes throughout the site. On some sites this may not be as much of an issue (although it still should be corrected), but given the small amount of content here it impacts the majority of the site content available.

Scope of the Evaluation

Website name
Timeclock Plus
Scope of the website
Review includes the homepage along with pages chosen to provide the greatest range of content types throughout the site. At least one random page will also be included in the review.
WCAG Version
2.1
Conformance target
A
Accessibility support baseline
Microsoft Edge, Opera, Safari, Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox
Additional evaluation requirements
Report will include an overview of all errors identified with examples where needed.
