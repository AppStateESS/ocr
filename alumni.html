Executive Summary

There are some issues here and there - mainly involving roles/titles for certain elements. Keyboard accessibility does have a major issue in that the main site menus are not accessible via keyboard at all.

Scope of the Evaluation

Website name
Alumni
Scope of the website
Review includes the homepage along with pages chosen to provide the greatest range of content types throughout the site. At least one random page will also be included in the review.
WCAG Version

Conformance target

Microsoft Edge, Opera, Safari, Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox
